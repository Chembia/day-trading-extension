🎯 Quick Start Guide

1. Understanding the Chart

Green candles = price went up
Red candles = price went down
Colored boxes = detected patterns

2. Viewing Patterns

• Hover over pattern in sidebar to highlight it on the chart
• Click pattern to zoom in on the chart
• Click "Read More" for a full explanation

3. Filtering Results

• Use "Filter by Type" to show specific patterns
• Adjust "Show Top N" to limit results
• Higher confidence = more reliable signal

4. Changing Settings

• Enter new stock symbol at the top control bar
• Change date range or interval
• Click "Re-Analyze" to fetch new data
